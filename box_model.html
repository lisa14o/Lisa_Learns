The Box Model

Padding

Extends the border surrounding the padding. Images, background, color are extended into padding so adding padding in a way extends the content area

Border

Starts at the end of padding and ends at the margin.

Margin

Extends the border area with empty space giving an element room between its neighbors

Margin Collapsing

When top and bottom margins are stacked, the margins are collapsed into one margin sized to the biggest margin of the two. It happens three different ways:

  • Adjacent Siblings
  • Parent and First/Last Child - there is no padding, border or inline content to separate the margin-top of a parent element from it's first child or margin-bottom from it's last child. The collapsed margin will appear outside of the parent container
  • Empty Blocks

Margin Collapsing effects negative margins as well. If the collapsing margins are 50px and -25px the margin would be 25px.
